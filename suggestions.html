Suggestions for COMI-1750

This class was very enjoyable for me. I liked the content and the projects. I was able to learn everything I needed for the class through the PowerPoint presentations posted on BlackBoard and through the book. However, I do really feel like I get more out of an in-person lecture than I do from anything else. I like that we were always able to email with any other questions we add, and office hours were always availiable, but there's just something about being in-person in a classroom and being able to raise your hand and get clairification on something in real time. I feel like I would've gotten more out of an in-person class.
